## Information We Collect and How We Use It

We collect and process information to run the app, provide your account and social features, send optional notifications, and improve reliability and safety.

**Account and profile**

- **Name or username** and **email address** when you create an account or sign in (including with **Google** or **Sign in with Apple**).
- **Profile photo** when you choose to upload one. You may use the **camera** or **photo library** only when you actively pick or capture an image; we use this to display your avatar in the app.

**Content you create**

- Posts, comments, reactions, and other content you submit, plus related metadata needed to show them in feeds and profiles.

**Push notifications**

- A **device token** (via **Firebase Cloud Messaging**) so we can deliver push notifications you opt into (for example follows, comments, or activity). You can turn notifications off in your device settings.

**Technical and security**

- Basic device/app diagnostics and identifiers as processed by our backend providers (see below), for example to protect against abuse, fix crashes, and keep the Service working.

We **do not** ask you for **gender**. We **do not** continuously collect your **precise GPS location** in LieMaster as part of this policy.
