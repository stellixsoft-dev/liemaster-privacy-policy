# LieMaster — Account & data deletion

**Last updated:** April 30, 2026

---

## Overview

This page explains how to **delete your LieMaster account** and what happens to your information afterward. For general data practices, see our **Privacy Policy**.

LieMaster is published by the same developer as shown on the **Google Play** store listing for **LieMaster**.

---

## Delete your account in the app (primary method)

1. Open **LieMaster** on your device and sign in.  
2. Go to **Settings**.  
3. Tap **Delete Account**.  
4. Read the warning, then continue and confirm when prompted.  
5. **Confirm your identity**, depending on how you signed up:  
   - **Email and password:** enter your password.  
   - **Google:** complete **Sign in with Google** when asked.  
   - **Apple (where available):** complete **Sign in with Apple** when asked.  
6. When deletion succeeds, you will be signed out and your **login cannot be used again** for that account.

Deletion is **permanent** for the authentication account. You may need to **type `DELETE`** where the app asks you to, to confirm.

---

## What we delete or disable when you delete your account

When you complete in-app account deletion, we **intend** to:

- **Remove your ability to sign in** by deleting the **Firebase Authentication** user tied to your account.  
- **Clear** the **push notification device token** associated with your account (so we should not target that account for new pushes).  
- **Release your reserved username/handle** in our username registry **when applicable**, so it is no longer tied to your account in that system.

---

## What may be kept or delayed

Some information **may not be instantly erased everywhere**:

- **Posts, comments, votes, or other content** you already submitted **might remain** in our databases or storage (for example so feeds or moderation records stay consistent) until we process them separately.  
- **Backups, caches, logs, or copies** held by us or our providers (**Firebase / Google Cloud**, etc.) may persist for a **limited time** under normal operations and then age out.  
- **Aggregated or de-identified** data that does not identify you may be retained for security, statistics, or legal compliance.

If you want **extra removal** of content tied to your old profile after account deletion, contact us using the email below and we will handle requests **where we can reasonably do so** and as required by applicable law.

## Delete via email (if you cannot use the app)

If you **cannot access the app** (for example you lost the device) but you still want your account deleted, email us from the **same address** you used to register, if possible:

**Email:** usamatahirsatti@gmail.com  

Include:

- The subject line: **LieMaster — Account deletion request**  
- Your **username** (if you remember it) and the **email** linked to the account  
- A short note that you want **account deletion** and (optional) removal of **specific content**

We may ask for reasonable steps to **verify** you own the account before we delete it.

---

## After deletion

You may **create a new account** later if the app allows it, but **previous content or history** may not be restored. If ads or device settings stored an **advertising ID**, you can reset or limit that in your **device settings** (separate from LieMaster).
